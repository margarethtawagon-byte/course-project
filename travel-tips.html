Travel Tips

A frequent greeting you will hear when on island is...

Hafa Adai!

Around the Island

  • Be ready for the heat; average high is around 29–30 °C (84–86 °F)
  • Island-time pace is slow and relaxed
  • Most establishments accept card
  • Look out for typhoon season (July–November)
  • Groceries may be expensive

Insider Tips

  • Himawari has good food for cheap
  • Check out the Sabalu Market every saturday morning for some good food and shopping.
  • Look out for our summer festivals: The Flame Tree Festival in April & the Taste of Marianas in May.

Safety & Etiquette

  • Be sure to always have a guide during hikes
  • Watch for dangerous currents
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen
  • Avoid jay-walking
  • Don’t litter
  • Respect the local culture
  • Do not leave belongings unattended
  • Beware of mosquitoes
