Welcome to Saipan!

Saipan is a tiny yet beautiful island in the Pacific. Here, you can enjoy the best tropical vacation of your life. From white sand beaches to exquisite landscape, Saipan offers the full experience. Not only is the island beautiful itself, it is filled with World War II history and the local cultures of Chamorro and Carolinian. It is a trip that you will not regret taking.

On this website, you can find everything you need to plan your trip to Saipan. It features restaurants, cafes, different activities from swimming to hiking, as well as some important tips that will set you up for success on your trip to our beautiful island!
